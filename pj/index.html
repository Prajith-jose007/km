Functions Of The Organization

Since its inception the organization has been focused on charity, housing schemes, financial support for marriage, healthcare assistance, educational support, inducing art and cultural activities, traveling assistance for immigrants who require it, and also activities for the growth and benefits for the women.

From the beginning itself the Head office of the organization in Karakonam , Thiruvanthapuram in order to focus its operation in India.

Furthermore, in order to achieve economic growth and increase work opportunities in the country itself, the job and development committee was able to establish the PRAVASI FASHION GARMENTS and begin its operations.

In order to further enhance the functioning and operations, the organization has been divided into various departments.
